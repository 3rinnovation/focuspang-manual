# 5. Settings

## How to change your password

As an administrator, you can change your account password to keep your account secure.

Click the gear icon in the top left corner to access your account settings and change your password.

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/admin/05-01.jpg" size="lg" />

:::tip Security tip
Change your password regularly to keep your administrator account secure. Use a strong password that includes letters, numbers, and special characters.
:::
