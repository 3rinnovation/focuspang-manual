# Assignments

Your teacher will give you assignments to help you practice what you've learned. Let's learn how to check and submit your assignments using focuspang.

## What are assignments?

Assignments are like homework that your teacher gives you to do. They help you:

- Practice what you learned in class
- Show your teacher what you know
- Get better at the subject

Your teacher might give you different types of assignments like reading, writing, or solving problems.

### Step 2: Look at what you need to do

1. Look at the assignments that your teacher has given you
2. Click on the assignment you want to work on

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/student/08-01.jpg" size="lg" />

### Step 3: Submit your work

3. When you click the **[Submit]** button, you'll see:

   - A place to write your assignment title
   - An **[Upload Assignment File]** button to add your work

   After you write the title and upload your file, the **[Submit Complete]** button will become active.

### Step 4: Send it to your teacher

4. Click the **[Submit Complete]** button to submit your assignment to your teacher

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/student/08-02.jpg" size="lg" />

:::tip Tips for doing assignments well

- Read the assignment instructions carefully before starting
- Finish your work before the deadline
- Check that your file uploaded correctly before submitting
- Ask your teacher if you need help understanding the assignment
  :::

## What to do if you have problems

If you can't upload your file or have trouble submitting, ask your teacher for help. They can show you how to do it or help fix any problems.
