# Assignments

## What are assignments?

Assignments are homework or projects that your teacher gives you to complete.
You can check your assignments in focuspang and submit your completed work.

## How to check and submit assignments

### Step 1: View your assignments

1. Look at the assignments that your teacher has given you

### Step 2: Open an assignment

2. Click on the assignment you want to work on

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/student/08-01.jpg" size="lg" />

### Step 3: Submit your work

3. When you click the **[Submit]** button, you'll see:

   - A place to write your assignment title
   - An **[Upload Assignment File]** button to add your work

   After you write the title and upload your file, the **[Submit Complete]** button will become active.

### Step 4: Complete your submission

4. Click the **[Submit Complete]** button to submit your assignment to your teacher

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/student/08-02.jpg" size="lg" />

:::tip Tips for assignments

- Make sure to read the assignment instructions carefully
- Complete your work before the deadline
- Check that your file uploaded correctly before submitting
  :::
