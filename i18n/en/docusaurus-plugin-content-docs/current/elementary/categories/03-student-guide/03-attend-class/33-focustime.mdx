# Focus Time

Focus Time is when you have your actual lesson with your teacher. It's like being in a regular classroom, but on your device! Let's learn how to join and participate in Focus Time.

## What is Focus Time?

Focus Time is your real lesson time with your teacher. During Focus Time:

- You can see what your teacher is showing on their screen
- You can take notes by drawing on the screen
- Your teacher shares materials with you
- You can only use apps and websites that your teacher allows (this helps you focus on learning)

It's like having your teacher right next to you, helping you learn!

:::tip What can you do during Focus Time?

- Watch your teacher's screen during the lesson
- Take notes by drawing on the screen
- Get materials that your teacher shares
- Use only the apps and websites your teacher chooses for the lesson
  :::

## Tips for Focus Time

- Pay attention to what your teacher is showing
- Take notes when something is important
- Ask questions if you don't understand
- Use only the apps your teacher allows
- Have fun learning with your teacher and classmates!
