# Focus Time

## What is Focus Time?

Focus Time is when you have your actual lesson with your teacher.
When your teacher starts Focus Time, you can see your teacher's screen and participate in the lesson.

:::tip What can you do during Focus Time?

- Watch your teacher's screen during the lesson
- Take notes by drawing on the screen
- Receive materials that your teacher shares
- Only use apps and websites that your teacher allows
  :::
