# Group Activities

Group Activities is when your teacher puts you in small groups to work together on fun projects. It's like working with your friends to solve puzzles or create something together!

## What are Group Activities?

Group Activities is when your teacher divides the class into small groups to work together. During Group Activities:

- You work with a few classmates as a team
- You can share ideas and help each other
- You work together to complete projects or solve problems
- Everyone gets to participate and contribute

It's like having a team of friends to work with on schoolwork!

## Tips for working well in groups

:::tip How to be a great group member

- Be kind and respectful to your group members
- Listen carefully to what others have to say
- Share your own ideas too
- Help each other when someone needs it
- Work together as a team to succeed
- Have fun learning together!
  :::

## What makes a good group member?

A good group member:

- Listens to others
- Shares their own ideas
- Helps when someone needs it
- Is kind and patient
- Works hard to help the group succeed
