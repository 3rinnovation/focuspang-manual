# Quizpang

## What is Quizpang?

Quizpang is when your teacher gives you fun quizzes to answer.
You can answer questions in real-time and see how you did compared to your classmates.

## How to participate in Quizpang

### Go to Quizpang

üìç **Main Page** ‚Üí **Click your class in the timetable** ‚Üí **Click [Quizpang] in the left sidebar**

### Step 1: Wait for your teacher to start

1. When your teacher starts a quiz, it will begin immediately on your screen

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/student/10-01.jpg" size="lg" />

### Step 2: Answer the questions

Answer the questions as quickly and accurately as you can within the time limit your teacher set.

### Step 3: See your results

2. When the time limit is over, you'll automatically see your ranking compared to your classmates

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/student/10-02.jpg" size="lg" />

### Step 4: Return to the main screen

3. Click the **[Close]** button to go to the quiz results page and return to the main screen

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/student/10-03.jpg" size="lg" />

:::tip Tips for Quizpang

- Read the questions carefully before answering
- Try to answer quickly but don't rush and make mistakes
- Have fun competing with your classmates!
  :::
