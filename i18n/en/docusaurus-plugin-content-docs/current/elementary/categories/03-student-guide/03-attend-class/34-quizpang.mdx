# Quizpang

Quizpang is a fun quiz game where you can answer questions your teacher creates. It's like playing a game while learning! Let's learn how to play Quizpang.

## What is Quizpang?

Quizpang is a fun way to answer questions about what you've learned. When you play Quizpang:

- Your teacher creates questions about your lessons
- You can answer questions in real-time
- You can see how you did compared to your classmates
- It's like playing a learning game with your friends!

:::tip Tips for doing well in Quizpang

- Read the questions carefully before answering
- Try to answer quickly but don't rush and make mistakes
- Remember what you learned in class
- Have fun competing with your classmates!
- Don't worry if you get some wrong - that's how you learn!
  :::

## What to do after Quizpang

After playing Quizpang, you can:

- Ask your teacher about questions you got wrong
- Review the material you missed
- Get ready for the next quiz
- Be proud of what you learned!
