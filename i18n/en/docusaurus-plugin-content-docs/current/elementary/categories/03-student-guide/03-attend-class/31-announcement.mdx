# Class Announcements

## What are class announcements?

Class announcements are messages that your teacher sends to you and your classmates.
These messages might tell you about homework, upcoming tests, or other important information about your class.

### Read your teacher's messages

Your teacher uses announcements to share important information about your class. Check them regularly so you don't miss anything important!
