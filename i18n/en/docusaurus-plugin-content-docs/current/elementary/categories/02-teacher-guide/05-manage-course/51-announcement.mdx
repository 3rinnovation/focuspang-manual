# Class Announcements

## What are class announcements?

Class announcements are messages you send to your students through focuspang. You can use them to:

- Share important information about upcoming lessons
- Remind students about homework or projects
- Send encouragement or congratulations
- Notify students about schedule changes

## How to create an announcement

### Step 1: Go to the announcements section

üìç Main Page ‚Üí Click your class in the timetable ‚Üí Click [Announcement] in the left sidebar

### Step 2: Start creating your announcement

Click the **[+Class Announcement]** button to open the announcement creation screen.

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/04-01.jpg" size="lg" />

### Step 3: Write your announcement

Fill in the following information:

- **Title**: A short, clear title for your announcement
- **Content**: Your message to students
- **When to publish**: Choose when students will see it

### Step 4: Choose when to publish

You have two options:

- **Immediately**: Students will see the announcement right away
- **Schedule**: Set a specific date and time for the announcement to appear

### Step 5: Add extra content (optional)

You can make your announcement more helpful by adding:

- Images or photos
- File attachments
- Links to resources

### Step 6: Send to multiple classes (optional)

If you want to send the same announcement to several classes, click the [+ Add classes to announce] button to select additional classes.

### Step 7: Publish your announcement

When everything looks good, click the **[Register]** button to publish your announcement.

:::tip What happens next?
Once you publish an announcement, students can see it in their focuspang app. They'll get a notification so they don't miss important information.
:::
