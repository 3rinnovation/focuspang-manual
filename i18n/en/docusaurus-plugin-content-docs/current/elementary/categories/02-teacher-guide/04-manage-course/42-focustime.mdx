### App/Web Control

You can manage the apps and websites used by students in your class.

1. click [Apps and websites you want to control student use] and then click the **Add App/Web Control Settings** button.

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/05-05.jpg" size="lg" />

2. Select the sites you want to restrict access to and click the **[Done]** button, and the selected sites will be automatically blocked from students.
   To unblock, click the checkbox of the app/website again to deselect it from the control list.
