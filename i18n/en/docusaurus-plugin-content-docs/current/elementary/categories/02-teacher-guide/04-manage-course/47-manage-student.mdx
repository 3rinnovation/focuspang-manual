# Student Management

## What is student management?

Student management lets you view and manage the students in your class.
You can edit student information or remove students from your class roster when needed.

## How to manage students

### Go to student management

📍 **Main Page** → **Click your class in the timetable** → **Click [Student List] in the left sidebar**

You can see all the students in your class and manage their information.

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/08-01.jpg" size="lg" />

### Edit student information

Click the ✏️**pencil icon** next to a student's name to edit their student number and name.

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/08-02.jpg" size="lg" />

### Remove a student from class

Click the 🚪**door-shaped icon** to remove that student from your class roster.

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/08-03.jpg" size="lg" />

:::tip When to use student management

- When a student's information needs to be corrected
- When a student transfers to a different class
- When you need to update the class roster
  :::
