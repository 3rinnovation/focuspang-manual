# Class Announcements

1. Click the **[+Class Announcement]** button to go to the Create New Announcement screen.

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/04-01.jpg" size="lg" />

2. Enter a title, when the announcement will be published, and content, and click the **[Register]** button to register the announcement.

   :::tip

   - You can **set when to publish the announcement**.

     - Immediately: The announcement will be visible to students as soon as you register it.

     - Schedule: You can schedule the announcement to be made public by specifying a date and time of your choice.

   - By clicking the [+ Add classes to announce] button, you can **announce** what you're creating to multiple classes at the same time.

   - You can also add images, photos, and attachments to your announcement.

   - Registered announcements can be viewed on the student app/web.

   :::
