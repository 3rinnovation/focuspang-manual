# Group Activities

## What are group activities?

Group activities let you organize your students into small groups for collaborative learning.
You can create topics for group work and monitor how each group is doing during lessons.

## How to create group activities

### Go to group activities

üìç **Main Page** ‚Üí **Click your class in the timetable** ‚Üí **Click [Group Activities] in the left sidebar**

### Step 1: Create a new group activity

1. Click the **[+Create New Group Activity Topic]** button to open the group creation window

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/09-01.jpg" size="lg" />

### Step 2: Set up your groups

2. Fill in the following information:
   - **Group activity topic**: What the groups will work on
   - **Number of people per group**: How many students in each group
   - Click **[Random Assignment]** to automatically organize the groups

:::tip Group management options

- **Change specific students**: If you want to move only certain students to different groups, you can change their group assignment
- **Manual assignment**: If you want to create groups yourself, use random assignment first, then change individual students to your preferred groups
  :::

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/09-02.jpg" size="lg" />

### Step 3: View group details

3. Once the groups are created, click the **[View Group Details]** button to see which students are in each group

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/09-03.jpg" size="lg" />
<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/09-04.jpg" size="lg" />

### Step 4: Monitor groups during Focus Time

4. During Focus Time, students will be grouped together by their assigned groups, and you can monitor each group separately

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/09-05.jpg" size="lg" />

:::tip Using group activities effectively
Group activities work best when combined with Focus Time, allowing you to see how each group is collaborating and participating in the lesson.
:::
