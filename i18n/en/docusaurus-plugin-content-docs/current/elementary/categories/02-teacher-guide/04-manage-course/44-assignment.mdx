## Registering an Assignment

### Step 1: Create a New Assignment

Click the **[+ Add Assignment]** button to go to the assignment registration page.

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/44-01.png" size="lg" />

### Step 2: Enter Assignment Details

Fill in the following fields and click [Register]:

- **Assignment Title**
- **Assignment Content**: Provide detailed instructions for students.
- **Visibility**: Choose when students can see the assignment.
- **Due Date**: Set the assignment deadline.
- **Attachments**: Attach any files that help students understand the assignment.

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/44-02.png" size="lg" />
<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/44-03.png" size="lg" />

### Step 3: Check Student Submissions

1. In the Assignment Management list, check **submission status and whether grading** is complete. Click **[View Details]** on the assignment you want to check.

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/44-08.png" size="lg" />

2. In the [**Student Submission Status**] tab, you can see the list of students.

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/44-09.png" size="lg" />

3. Click a studentâ€™s name to view the submitted assignment content.

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/44-10.png" size="lg" />

### Step 4: Provide Feedback

Enter scores and comments, then click [**Save Feedback**]. Students can view the feedback once saved.

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/44-11.png" size="lg" />

## Editing and Deleting Assignments

### Editing Assignments

1. In the Assignment Management list, click [**View Details**].

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/44-04.png" size="lg" />

2. Click [Edit].

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/44-05.png" size="lg" />

3. Make changes and click [**Save**] to submit the updates.

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/44-06.png" size="lg" />

### Deleting Assignments

In the Assignment Management list, click [**Delete**] to remove the assignment.

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/44-07.png" size="lg" />
