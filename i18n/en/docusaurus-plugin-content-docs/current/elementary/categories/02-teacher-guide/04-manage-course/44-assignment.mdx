### Create

1. Click the **[+Add Assignment]** button to go to the new assignment enrollment screen.

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/07-01.jpg" size="lg" />

2. Create a title, when the assignment will be available, and content, then create the assignment.

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/07-02.jpg" size="lg" />

### Edit and Delete

You can edit a registered assignment by clicking [More] in the [Manage assignments] list, or delete an assignment by clicking the [Delete] button.

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/07-03.jpg" size="lg" />

### Viewing assignments

Click [More] in the [Manage Assignments] list to check the status of your students' assignment submissions and download the submitted assignments.

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/07-04.jpg" size="lg" />
