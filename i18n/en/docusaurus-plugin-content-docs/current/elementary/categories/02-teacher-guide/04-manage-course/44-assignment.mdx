# Assignment Management

## What is Assignment Management?

Assignment Management is a feature that lets you give homework or assignments to your students and receive their submissions.
You can check which students have submitted their work and download the files they've submitted.

:::tip What can you do with Assignment Management?

- Give assignments to your students
- Check which students have submitted their work
- Download submitted assignment files
- Edit or delete assignments you've created
  :::

## How to use Assignment Management

### Go to Assignment Management

📍 **Main Page** → **Click your class in the timetable** → **Click [Assignment Management] in the left sidebar**

### Step 1: Create a new assignment

1. Click the **[+Add Assignment]** button to go to the assignment creation screen

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/07-01.jpg" size="lg" />

### Step 2: Write your assignment details

2. Fill in the following information:
   - **Assignment title**: The name students will see
   - **Assignment content**: Detailed instructions for what students need to do
   - **When to publish**: Choose when students will see the assignment

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/07-02.jpg" size="lg" />

### Step 3: Edit or delete assignments

If you want to change or remove an assignment you've created:

1. In the [Assignment Management] list, click the **[More]** button
2. To **edit**: You can change the assignment content
3. To **delete**: Click the [Delete] button

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/07-03.jpg" size="lg" />

### Step 4: Check student submissions

To see which students have submitted their assignments:

1. In the [Assignment Management] list, click the **[More]** button
2. You can see which students have submitted their work
3. You can **download** the submitted assignment files
