2. Enter verification code
3. Enter name and password
4. Agree to terms and select affiliated school

You do not need to submit a separate privacy agreement because we collect and disclose your personal information to third parties when you sign up.

:::warning Account approval

After signing up, the **account approval** must be completed by your school's designated **administrator** before you can use the account.\
For faster approval processing, please request approval directly from your administrator.

:::
