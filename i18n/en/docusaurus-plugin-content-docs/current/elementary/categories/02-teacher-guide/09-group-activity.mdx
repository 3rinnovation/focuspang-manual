# 9. Group Activities

1. Click the [+Create New Group Activity Topic] button to enter a group activity topic.

<Image src="/img/kenr/elementary/teacher/09-01.jpg" size="lg" />

2. Enter the group topic, set the number of members per group, then click random selection to divide students into groups and save.

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/09-02.jpg" size="lg" />

3. Once groups are created, click the [View Group Details] button to check the student list assigned to each group.

4. Students are grouped by teams in [Focus Time], allowing you to monitor and distinguish each group individually.
