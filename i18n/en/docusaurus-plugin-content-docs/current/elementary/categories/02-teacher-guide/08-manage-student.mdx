# 8. Student Management

You can edit students' student numbers and names, or delete students from the list.

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/08-01.jpg" size="lg" />

### Editing

Click the ✏️pencil icon to edit the student's student number and name.

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/08-02.jpg" size="lg" />

### Removing

Click the 🚪door-shaped icon to remove that student from the class.

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/08-03.jpg" size="lg" />
