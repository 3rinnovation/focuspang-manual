# 2. Sign Up / Login

## Why do you need a focuspang account?

As a teacher, you'll use focuspang to manage your digital classroom. With your account, you can:

- Create and manage your classes
- Share your screen with students
- Give quizzes and see results
- Monitor student attention during lessons

### Step 2: Complete the registration process

Follow these steps to create your account:

1. **Verify your email**: Enter your email address and check for a verification code
2. **Enter the code**: Type the verification code you received in your email
3. **Set up your profile**: Enter your name and create a password
4. **Choose your school**: Select your school from the list and agree to the terms
5. **Finish**: Click the [Confirm] and [Next] buttons to complete registration

:::tip Privacy policy
You don't need to submit a separate privacy agreement - you'll agree to our privacy policy during the sign-up process.
:::

### Step 3: Wait for approval

After signing up, your school's administrator needs to approve your account before you can use it.

:::tip Getting approved faster
Contact your school's IT administrator or the person who manages focuspang at your school to approve your account quickly.
:::

## How to log in

Once your account is approved, you can log in with your email and password.
