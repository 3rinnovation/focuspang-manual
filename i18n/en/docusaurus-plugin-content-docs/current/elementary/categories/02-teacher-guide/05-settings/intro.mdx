# 5. Edit Information

On the home screen, click your profile picture or name in the top left corner to go to the **Settings page**.

- Click the [+] button next to your profile picture to **add and change your profile picture**.
- Enter the name or email you want to edit, then click the **Change information** button to save your changes.
- To change your password, first enter your current password. Then click the **[Next]** button to enter a new password and click the **[Change Password]** button to finalize the password change.
