# Choosing Your Role (Homeroom / Subject Teacher)

In focusang, the features available depend on your role. Setting your role correctly helps you use focuspang more effectively.

## Difference Between Roles

### Homeroom Teacher

- Manages the entire class
- Can oversee all subjects for students
- Creates and manages classes by homeroom

### Subject Teacher

- Teaches only specific subjects
- Can teach the same subject across multiple classes
- Creates and manages classes by subject

## How to Change Your Role

### Step 1: Go to Settings

Click your profile picture or name at the top left of the home screen.

### Step 2: Select Role

Switch between Homeroom Teacher and Subject Teacher as needed.

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/10-02.jpg" size="lg" />

:::tip Which Role Should I Choose?

- **Homeroom Teacher**: If you teach all subjects to one class
- **Subject Teacher**: If you teach one subject across several classes (e.g., English, Music, Art)
  :::
