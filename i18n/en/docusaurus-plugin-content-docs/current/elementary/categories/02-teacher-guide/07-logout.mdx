# 7. Logout

## When should you log out?

You should log out when:

- You're finished teaching for the day
- You're switching to a different teacher account
- You're using a shared computer or device

## How to log out

### Step 1: Go to settings

1. On the home screen, click your profile picture or name in the upper left corner to go to the settings page

### Step 2: Log out

2. Click the **[Logout]** button to log out

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/teacher/11-01.jpg" size="lg" />

## Important reminder

:::tip Why logging out is important

- **Always log out** after your classes are finished
- If you don't log out, you might have **duplicate login sessions**
- This can cause the student monitoring feature to **not work properly**
- Logging out keeps your account secure and ensures focuspang works correctly
  :::
