# 5. Settings

You can securely and efficiently operate the system by managing your administrator account.

## Changing Your Password

###How to change your password

Click the **gear icon** at the top left to change your account password.

<Image src="/img/en/elementary/admin/05-01.jpg" size="lg" />

### Guide to Creating a Secure Password

#### Password creation rules

- **Length**: At least 8 characters (12+ recommended)
- **Complexity**: Combination of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters
- **Uniqueness**: Different from passwords used on other sites
- **Unpredictability**: Not related to personal information

#### Passwords to avoid

- **Personal information**: name, date of birth, phone number, etc.
- **Sequential characters**: 123456, abcdef
- **Keyboard patterns**: qwerty, asdf
- **Common words**: password, admin

#### Examples

- **Good**: MySchool2024!@, Fp#Admin789
- **Bad**: 123456, password, HongGilDong1234

## Best Practices for Settings Management

### Regular checks

- **Monthly**: Verify and update account information
- **Quarterly**: Change your password
- **Semiannually**: Review overall security settings
- **Annually**: Recheck system environment settings

### Security tips

- **Use a password manager**: Safely create and store passwords
- Enable **two-factor authentication** whenever possible
- **Regularly check logs**: Monitor unusual login attempts

### Preventive measures

- **Keep information up to date**: Immediately update when personal details change
- **Test after changes**: Ensure proper operation after adjusting settings
- **Backup plan**: Prepare alternative plans in case the administrator is unavailable
