# 4. Logout

After finishing a class or using focuspang, always make sure to log out.
Proper logout is essential for protecting your personal information and ensuring smooth operation for the next class.

## How to Log Out

### Step 1: Go to the Settings Page

On the home screen, click **your profile picture or name** in the top-left corner to open the settings page.

### Step 2: Execute Logout

Click the [**Logout**] button on the settings page to log out.

<Image src="/img/en/middlehigh/student/12-01.jpg" size="lg" />

## Always Remember to Log Out Make sure to log out in the following situations:

**1. After Classes End of each class:**

- Log out immediately when a class ends.
- Breaks: Before a long break or lunchtime.
- Before going home: After using the school device.

**2. When Security is Needed**

- Leaving your seat: Even for a short time.
- After using a friend’s device: If you borrowed someone else’s device.

## Common Issues and Solutions

### If you cannot log out:

1. **Force close the app**: Exit the app completely and reopen it.
2. **Restart your device**: Restart your smartphone or tablet.
3. **Check network connection**: Ensure you are connected to the internet.
4. **Ask your teacher**: If the problem persists, request assistance from your teacher.

### If duplicate login occurs:

1. **Log out from all devices**: Make sure you are logged out from all devices you used.
2. **Wait a few minutes**: Wait 5–10 minutes before logging in again.
3. **Use only one device**: Log in on only one device at a time.
4. **Ask your teacher for help**: Contact your teacher if the issue continues.
