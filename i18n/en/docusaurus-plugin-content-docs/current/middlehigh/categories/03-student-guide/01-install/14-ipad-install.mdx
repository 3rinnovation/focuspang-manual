# Install focuspang on iPad

## What we're going to do

We're going to install the focuspang app on your iPad so you can use it in class with your teacher.
This guide will show you exactly how to do it step by step.

## Before we start

Make sure you have:

- Your iPad
- Internet connection
- About 5 minutes of time

## Step 1: Open the App Store

Find and tap the **App Store** icon on your iPad.

## Step 2: Search for focuspang

In the search box, type "focuspang" to find the app.

## Step 3: Install the app

Tap the "Get" or "Install" button to download the focuspang app to your iPad.

## Step 4: Open the app

Once the installation is complete, tap the focuspang app icon on your home screen to open it.

## You're all done!

Great job! You've successfully installed focuspang on your iPad.
If you have any trouble installing or if the screen looks different from these instructions, ask your teacher for help.
