## What is a Group Activity?

A Group Activity is a session where the teacher divides the class into small groups to work together.
During a Group Activity, you can:

- Work as a team with several classmates.
- Share ideas and help each other.
- Solve problems or create projects together.
- Make sure everyone participates and contributes.

## How to Participate

### Step 1: Check Your Group

Click the [**View Group Details**] button to see which group you belong to.

### Step 2: Meet Your Group Members

Find out your group number and see who else is in the same group.
