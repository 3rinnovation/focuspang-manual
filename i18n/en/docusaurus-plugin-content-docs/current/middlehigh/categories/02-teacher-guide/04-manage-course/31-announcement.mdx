# Class Announcements

#### üìç Main Page ‚Üí Click the class in the timetable ‚Üí Click [Announcement] in the left sidebar

1. Click the **[+Class Announcement]** button to go to the Create New Announcement screen.

<Image src="/img/en/middlehigh/teacher/03-01.jpg" size="lg" />

2. Enter a title, when to publish the announcement, and content, and click the **[Register]** button to register the announcement.

   :::info

   - You can **set when to publish the announcement**.

     - Immediately: The announcement will be visible to students as soon as you register it.

     - Schedule: You can schedule the announcement to be made public by specifying a date and time of your choice.

   - By clicking the [+ Add classes to announce] button, you can **announce** what you're creating to multiple classes at the same time.

   - You can also add images, photos, and attachments to your announcement.

   - Registered announcements can be viewed on the student app/web.

   :::

<Image src="/img/en/middlehigh/teacher/03-02.jpg" size="lg" />

### Editing and Deleting

1. To edit an announcement, click the **[View Content]** button on the right side of the announcement in the announcement list to go to the details screen.

2. To delete an announcement, click the **[Delete]** button on the right side of the announcement you want to delete in the announcement list.

<Image src="/img/en/middlehigh/teacher/03-03.jpg" size="lg" />

:::tip

In the announcement details screen, you can check the list of students who have read and haven't read the announcement.

:::
