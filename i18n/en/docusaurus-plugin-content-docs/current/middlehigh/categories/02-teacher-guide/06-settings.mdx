# 4. Editing Information

On the home screen, click your profile picture or name in the top left corner to go to the **Settings page**.

- Click the **[+]** button next to your profile picture to **add or change your profile picture**.
- Edit your name or email as desired, then click the **Update** button to save your changes.
- To change your password, first enter your current password. Then click the **[Next]** button to enter a new password and click the **[Change Password]** button to complete the password change.
